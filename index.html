What we do

Our services

From AI strategy and MLOps to data lakehouse builds and cloud migrations — we design, engineer, and operate the intelligent data systems that modern businesses compete on.

01 / AI FIRST
Generative AI & LLM Integration
Build production-ready AI applications on your data — RAG pipelines, LLM-powered APIs, prompt engineering, and AI agent workflows. We connect your enterprise data to the latest foundation models securely and at scale.
02 / AI/ML
MLOps & AI Platform Engineering
Operationalize your AI/ML workloads end-to-end — feature stores, model training pipelines, serving infrastructure, drift monitoring, and retraining automation. Reliable AI, not just experiments.
03 / AI STRATEGY
AI Readiness & Data Strategy
Not sure where to start with AI? We assess your data maturity, identify the highest-value AI use cases, and build a clear roadmap — from raw data to intelligent decision-making.
04 / LAKEHOUSE
Data Lakehouse Engineering
Design and build production-grade Databricks platforms on AWS or Azure. Medallion architecture, semantic model refresh, event-driven pipelines, and full CI/CD from day one.
05 / ETL
ETL & Pipeline Development
Reliable, observable data pipelines using PySpark, dbt, Azure Data Factory, and more. We handle both real-time streaming and batch workloads at enterprise scale.
06 / CLOUD
Cloud Data Architecture
Multi-cloud expertise across AWS (S3, Glue, Redshift) and Azure (Synapse, ADF, SQL DW). Migrations, lift-and-shift, and greenfield builds with infrastructure-as-code.
07 / QUALITY
Data Quality & Governance
Monitoring frameworks, SLA enforcement, unit testing, and schema governance. SAP integrations (S/4HANA, CC) and dbt transformations for high-integrity data estates.
08 / DEVOPS
DataOps & CI/CD
GitHub Actions, Terraform, dbx, and semantic-release pipelines. Python packaging, secret management, and secure infrastructure provisioning for repeatable deployments.
09 / CONSULTING
Expert Consulting & Advisory
Fractional data leadership, architecture reviews, team training, and hands-on mentoring. Whether you need a strategic sparring partner or embedded expertise — we're in your corner from day one.

How we work

Our approach

We believe great AI and data engineering is equal parts architecture discipline, code quality, and business context. Here's how a typical engagement unfolds.

01
Discover & scope
We audit your current stack, understand your business goals, and map the data flows that matter most.
02
Design & architect
We propose a solution architecture — choosing the right tools, cloud services, and data models for your specific context.
03
Build & test
Iterative delivery with CI/CD from the start. Every pipeline is tested, monitored, and documented before it goes to production.
04
Scale & hand over
We scale your platform, train your team, and provide clear runbooks — so you own and operate it confidently.

About Datalaya

Built by engineers, for builders

Datalaya.ai was founded with a simple conviction: that great data engineering — done with craft, honesty, and deep technical rigour — can transform any organisation.

The name combines Data with laya (लय) — a Sanskrit word meaning rhythm, flow, and harmony. That's what we bring to your data: not just pipelines, but systems that breathe, scale, and deliver consistently.

We are headquartered in Turku, Finland, with the global mindset and cross-industry experience to serve clients across Europe and beyond. We are growing — and actively hiring engineers who share our standards.
